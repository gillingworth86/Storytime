Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-18

Storytime collects only the information needed to run our waitlist and communicate about product updates.

What we collect

  • Email address submitted through the waitlist form.
  • Basic analytics events (page views and signup interactions).

How we use data

  • Send confirmation and product-related email updates.
  • Measure landing page performance and improve messaging.
  • Understand aggregate demand during Phase 1 validation.

Third-party services

We currently use Kit (email collection) and Plausible (privacy-focused analytics). Their respective privacy policies also apply.

Your choices

  • You can unsubscribe from emails at any time via the unsubscribe link.
  • You can request deletion of your waitlist record by contacting us.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to hello@getstorytime.com.
